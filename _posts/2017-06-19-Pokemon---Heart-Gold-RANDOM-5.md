The most exciting news this time is that I'm right under the **400 Pok&eacute;mon** caught mark.

I've been considering completing the Pok&eacute;dex in this playthrough, only because it would definitely be doable.  When I ran the Randomizer, I enabled Catch 'em All Mode, which means I should be able to encounter every single Pok&eacute;mon species in the game.  Somewhere.  Now, I also had the Randomizer generate a log of the changes made, a fact which I have been abusing slightly by looking up locations for certain Pok&eacute;mon that I hadn't seen anywhere yet.

All that said, some Pok&eacute;mon, based on the log, seem only available in [swarms](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_outbreak), which is something that would be time consuming for me to hunt down since I believe I would have to wait a certain amount of time for the current swarm to change, and there would be guarantee that the new swarm is of a Pok&eacute;mon I needed.

So, completing the Pok&eacute;dex entirely might not happen, but I intend to get as close as possible to Catching 'em All!

Other than that, the last thing I have to do is defeat Red on Mt. Silver.  And he....is ***tough***.  I seem to remember *never* beating him when playing the original Pok&eacute;mon Gold Version, since his Pok&eacute;mon are just *so* highly levelled.

With the nifty fast-forward feature I am able to spam while emulating the game, I might be inclined to grind the 10 or so levels my Pok&eacute;mon probably need before they're ready to face him.

I guess we'll see how things turn out in the coming days.

One thing I *almost* forgot, I beat the Elite Four a 2nd time now.  I was using them to train, having a weaker Pok&eacute;mon hold an Exp Share, and went in fully intending to defeat maybe 2 of the Elite Four.  My team went all the way, much to my surprise!

![image]({{ site.url }}/images/heartgold1/5/halloffame.png)

The Golem you see there is the Exp Share holder.  That's another neat Randomizer feature, with the click of a button you make the trade-evolutions become level-based evolutions, in addition to making otherwise impossible evolutions possible.  Very helpful!

Thanks for reading!
